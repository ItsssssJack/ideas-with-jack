# Claw.md - Project Documentation

## Project Overview
This is the "Ideas with Jack" newsletter landing page - a modern, conversion-focused website built to showcase Jack Roberts' AI automation expertise and grow his email list.

## About Jack Roberts
- **7-Figure AI Automation Founder**: Built a successful AI automation business in 12 months
- **Community Leader**: Helps run the largest paid AI community in the world
- **Impact**: Has helped 4,000+ businesses scale with AI automation
- **Background**: Former tech startup founder with a successful exit, recognized as a Top-100 UK Entrepreneur

## Project Purpose
The landing page serves to:
1. Capture email subscribers interested in AI automation and business strategies
2. Showcase Jack's credibility and achievements
3. Provide value through the newsletter focused on practical AI implementations
4. Build a community of entrepreneurs learning about AI automation

## Key Features
- **Hero Section**: Email signup with social proof (25,000+ entrepreneurs)
- **About Section**: Personal story and credibility indicators with Jack's image
- **Email Preview**: Sample newsletter content to showcase value
- **Features Section**: Benefits of subscribing
- **Ecosystem Section**: Links to community and agency services
- **Footer**: Additional resources and social links

## Tech Stack
- React + TypeScript
- Vite (build tool)
- Tailwind CSS (styling)
- Lucide React (icons)

## Design Philosophy
- Dark mode aesthetic with yellow/gold accent colors
- Clean, modern, conversion-optimized layout
- Mobile-responsive design
- Subtle animations and hover effects
- Professional yet approachable tone

## Content Strategy
Jack's newsletter focuses on:
- Practical AI automations that actually work
- Business strategies from building a 7-figure agency
- Short, valuable, actionable content
- Real-world implementations (not theory)
